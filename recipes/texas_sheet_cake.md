# Texas Sheet Cake

## Ingredients

- 3 cups GF flour
- 3 tsp. Xanthun gum
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1 3/4 cups sugar
- 2 tsp. Baking soda
- 1 tsp. Salt
- 2/3 cup cocoa
- 3/4 cup vegetable oil
- 2 Tbsp vinegar
- 1 tsp. Vanilla
- 2 cups water

## Instructions

1. Mix wet. 
2. Mix dry. 
3. Add wet to dry. Mix. 
4. Grease pan with oil spray or use parchment paper and pour in mixture. 
5. Bake at 350 for 25-30 min
6. Put on frosting of choice.
