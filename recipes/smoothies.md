Breakfast

## Smoothies

### Ingredients

- 1 cup of liquid base (choose one)
    - almond milk
    - milk
    - fruit juice
- Spinach (make sure to mix this in with the milk)
- Chocolate/vanilla protein powder
- Vanilla yogurt
- Frozen fruit


### Instructions

1. In a blender add the liquid base
2. Add spinach (optional)
3. Add powder (optional)
4. Add yogurt
5. Add frozen fruit
6. Blend well
7. Enjoy!
