## Store-bought Granola with Yogurt

### Gluten Free Brands

- Kind
- Natures Path
- Bear Naked
- Udi's

### Ingredients

- Granola
- Yogurt (this is always gluten free)
- Fruit
    - Blueberry
    - Strawberry
    - Peach
    - Mango
    - Raspberry

### Instructions

1. Add to yogurt and your favorite fruit.
2. Enjoy!
